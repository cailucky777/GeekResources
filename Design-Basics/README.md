# Design Basics for Coders

> "If it works but looks terrible, users will assume it doesn't work."

Student projects often have great logic hidden behind a cluttered UI. A few design fundamentals take your work from "school assignment" to "portfolio piece."

## Why This Matters

- Your **capstone demo** is remembered for how it looks.
- A polished icon signals care on every phone that shows it.
- Good typography is invisible. Bad typography is all you see.
- Color choices affect how trustworthy and professional your app feels.

You don't need to be a designer — just to avoid a few common mistakes.
